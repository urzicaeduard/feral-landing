This is the pack.

FERAL was founded on one belief: speed isn't a feature, it's a philosophy. Every shoe, every kit, every fiber we engineer answers to a single question — does it make the next step faster than the last?

From the track to the street, our products live at the seam where sport meets culture. Worn by athletes who set the record and the kids who'll break it next.

Headquartered in Berlin, distributed in 120+ markets, partnered with athletes who refuse to slow down. The pack runs together. The pack runs first.

— Founded in 2026 by an obsessed runner and an engineer who kept losing to her. We're still chasing each other.
